## Lessons Applied
1. Tezos delivery work is multi-layered; contract, wallet, and data paths routinely depend on each other.
2. Contract/artifact drift and wallet/network mismatches are common upstream blockers.
3. Data indexing outcomes are only trustworthy once contract and wallet layers are stable.
4. Worker-style pipeline follow-through is required after protocol or contract behavior changes.
